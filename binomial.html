Chapter 7: Binomial Theorem – Formula Sheet

Binomial Expansion: \( (a + b)^n = \sum_{r=0}^{n} \binom{n}{r} a^{n-r} b^r \)
Binomial Coefficient: \( \binom{n}{r} = \frac{n!}{r!(n - r)!} \)
General Term: \( T_{r+1} = \binom{n}{r} a^{n-r} b^r \)
Middle Term(s):
  • If \( n \) is even: one middle term at \( r = \frac{n}{2} \)
  • If \( n \) is odd: two middle terms at \( r = \frac{n-1}{2} \) and \( r = \frac{n+1}{2} \)
Properties of Binomial Coefficients:
  • \( \binom{n}{r} = \binom{n}{n - r} \)
  • \( \binom{n}{0} + \binom{n}{1} + \dots + \binom{n}{n} = 2^n \)
  • Pascal’s Identity: \( \binom{n}{r} + \binom{n}{r - 1} = \binom{n + 1}{r} \)
