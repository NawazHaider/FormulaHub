Standard Form: \( z = x + iy \), where \( x, y \in \mathbb{R} \) and \( i^2 = -1 \)
Equality of Complex Numbers: \( z_1 = z_2 \iff x_1 = x_2 \text{ and } y_1 = y_2 \)
Modulus: \( |z| = \sqrt{x^2 + y^2} \)
Conjugate: \( \bar{z} = x - iy \)
Polar Form: \( z = r(\cos \theta + i \sin \theta) \), where \( r = |z| \) and \( \theta = \arg(z) \)
Multiplication in Polar Form: \( z_1 z_2 = r_1 r_2 [\cos(\theta_1 + \theta_2) + i \sin(\theta_1 + \theta_2)] \)
Division in Polar Form: \( \frac{z_1}{z_2} = \frac{r_1}{r_2} [\cos(\theta_1 - \theta_2) + i \sin(\theta_1 - \theta_2)] \)
