General Equation of a Conic: \( Ax^2 + Bxy + Cy^2 + Dx + Ey + F = 0 \)
Circle:
  • Standard form: \( (x - h)^2 + (y - k)^2 = r^2 \)
  • Center: \( (h, k) \), Radius: \( r \)
Parabola:
  • Standard form (horizontal): \( y^2 = 4ax \)
  • Standard form (vertical): \( x^2 = 4ay \)
  • Focus: \( (a, 0) \) or \( (0, a) \), Directrix: \( x = -a \) or \( y = -a \)
Ellipse:
  • Standard form: \( \frac{x^2}{a^2} + \frac{y^2}{b^2} = 1 \)
  • Center: \( (0, 0) \), Major axis along x if \( a > b \)
  • Foci: \( (\pm c, 0) \), where \( c^2 = a^2 - b^2 \)
Hyperbola:
  • Standard form: \( \frac{x^2}{a^2} - \frac{y^2}{b^2} = 1 \)
  • Center: \( (0, 0) \), Transverse axis along x
  • Foci: \( (\pm c, 0) \), where \( c^2 = a^2 + b^2 \)
Eccentricity:
  • Circle: \( e = 0 \)
  • Parabola: \( e = 1 \)
  • Ellipse: \( 0 < e < 1 \)
  • Hyperbola: \( e > 1 \)
