Factorial: \( n! = n \times (n - 1) \times (n - 2) \times \dots \times 1 \)
Permutations (when order matters): \( P(n, r) = nPr = \frac{n!}{(n - r)!} \)
Combinations (when order doesn't matter): \( C(n, r) = nCr = \frac{n!}{r!(n - r)!} \)
Properties of Combinations:
  • \( nCr = nC(n - r) \)
  • \( nC0 = nCn = 1 \)
  • \( nCr + nC(r - 1) = (n + 1)Cr \)
Permutations of Repeated Elements: \( \frac{n!}{p_1! \cdot p_2! \cdot \dots \cdot p_k!} \), where \( p_i \) are identical items
Circular Permutations:
  • Without repetition: \( (n - 1)! \)
  • With repetition: depends on symmetry and constraints
