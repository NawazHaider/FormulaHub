Mean (Average): \( \bar{x} = \frac{\sum x_i}{n} \), where \( x_i \) are observations and \( n \) is the number of observations
Mean of Grouped Data: \( \bar{x} = \frac{\sum f_i x_i}{\sum f_i} \), where \( f_i \) is frequency and \( x_i \) is mid-point
Median (Grouped Data): \( \text{Median} = l + \left( \frac{\frac{n}{2} - F}{f} \right) \cdot h \)
Where:
  • \( l \) = lower boundary of median class
  • \( F \) = cumulative frequency before median class
  • \( f \) = frequency of median class
  • \( h \) = class width
Mode (Grouped Data): \( \text{Mode} = l + \left( \frac{f_1 - f_0}{2f_1 - f_0 - f_2} \right) \cdot h \)
Where:
  • \( l \) = lower boundary of modal class
  • \( f_1 \) = frequency of modal class
  • \( f_0 \) = frequency before modal class
  • \( f_2 \) = frequency after modal class
  • \( h \) = class width
Variance: \( \sigma^2 = \frac{\sum (x_i - \bar{x})^2}{n} \)
Standard Deviation: \( \sigma = \sqrt{\frac{\sum (x_i - \bar{x})^2}{n}} \)
